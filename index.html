Introduction

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition. This portal provides all the necessary information and services related to GST.

Benefits of GST

GST has brought numerous benefits to the Indian economy:

  • Eliminates cascading effect of tax
  • Higher threshold for registration
  • Composition scheme for small businesses
  • Online simpler procedure
  • Defined treatment for E-commerce
  • Increased efficiency in logistics
  • Regulating the unorganized sector

How to Register for GST

Registering for GST is simple and can be done in a few steps:

  1. Visit the GST portal and click on 'Register Now'.
  2. Fill in the necessary details and submit the form.
  3. Receive an ARN (Application Reference Number) on your email and mobile.
  4. Upload the required documents.
  5. Receive your GST registration certificate.
